Here are the best books that i stumbled upon:

Philosophy:

The Millionaire Fastlane, MJ De Marco

A book with a really controversial title. It sounds like some "get rich quick scheme".
It seriously says that becoming rich fast is possible, brings up Bill Gates, Mark Zuckerberg and the author himself as examples of this possibility. It redefines wealth as a combination of health, relations and freedom and then focuses on showing a roadmap to freedom. "Get rich quick" is often criticized and seen as impossible, the blame should, as the author says, be put on it's bad brother, "get rich easy". Acquiring wealth is not easy, it takes hard work, constant learning and improvement, total commitment and several years to pass, still, several years is a relatively short period of time if what you get is a lifetime of freedom.
